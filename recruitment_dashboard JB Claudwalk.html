Conversion Signal Framework
High Probability Signals
  • Technical score ≥ 80
  • Behavior score ≥ 78
  • Manager score ≥ 79
  • Response within 3 days
  • Staff or Senior seniority
  • GitHub or Inbound source
  • Híbrido work mode preference
Persist with Caution
  • Technical score 70–80
  • No response after 7 days — try one more touch
  • Timing mismatch — follow up in 30–45 days
  • Pleno with strong domain experience
  • LinkedIn passive (screen well if engaged)
Low Conversion Signals
  • No response after 10+ days and 3 touches
  • Technical score below 70
  • Low engagement flagged by recruiter
  • Salary mismatch surfaced early
  • Indicação with no warm intro context
  • Junior + Remote combination
When to Persist
  • High scorer who missed first outreach
  • Timing mismatch — nurture and revisit
  • Headcount closed — hold in talent pool
  • LinkedIn passive with matching senior profile
  • Strong referrer endorsement with context
8 Evidence-Based Recommendations
Critical
01
Fix the no-response bottleneck first
34.9% of the pipeline — 210 candidates — never responds. Deploy a 3-touch outreach sequence with variation in channel, timing, and message framing. A/B test subject lines by source. This single fix could unlock 30–50 additional hires at current downstream conversion rates.
Recover 30–50 potential hires from lost pipeline
High
02
Double down on GitHub and Inbound
GitHub (11%) and Inbound (10.1%) far outperform the 8.2% average. Allocate more sourcing hours to GitHub for technical roles. Invest in employer branding to grow organic Inbound volume.
+3–5% hire rate on targeted requisitions
High
03
Audit and gate the Indicação (Referral) process
Referrals rank last at 4.7%. Introduce mandatory pre-qualification: referrers must confirm role fit, compensation range, and availability before any name enters the pipeline.
Raise Indicação from 4.7% toward industry avg of ~12%
High
04
Fast-track high scorers (≥80 composite) within 24h

Medium
05
Capture and share top performers' playbook
Bruno (15.3%) and Ana (13.3%) convert at 4× the rate of bottom performers. Run structured retrospectives — examine outreach scripts, channel mix, and evaluation criteria. Document and share as team playbooks.
Raise team average from 8.2% toward 12%+
Medium
06
Build a talent nurture pool from timing and headcount rejections
24 candidates were rejected for timing mismatch and 22 for headcount closure — not performance failures. Tag in ATS, set a 60-day re-engagement reminder, and contact them first when requisitions reopen.
Zero-cost pipeline recovery for future openings
Medium
07
Pilot AI-assisted pre-scoring before first outreach
Use AI tools to score LinkedIn and GitHub profiles against role criteria before sending the first message — filtering by signals correlated with hire: seniority, relevant work history, tech stack alignment.

Ongoing

Establish a monthly recruiter performance review cadence
Track hire rate, response rate, and time-to-hire per recruiter every month. Build a shared dashboard visible to the full team. Use data to guide coaching conversations — not for blame, but for growth.
